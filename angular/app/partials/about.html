About

Unwrong Ltd is in equal parts a boutique software development company and a digital tinkerer's toolshed for brothers Ed & Rob Silverton.

Established in 2004, we've been passionate about creating cutting-edge web/mobile experiences ever since, have worked on many commercial projects and have released a number of open source libraries.
